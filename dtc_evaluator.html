Evaluating Private Genetic Testing

Private genetic tests are everywhere now. Some you can buy online with a kit. Some come from private clinics where a doctor orders the test. They all promise to tell you something about yourself. But are they worth it?

Pick one of the three types of testing below. You will see pages from a made-up company that sells that kind of test, then answer five questions to evaluate it. At the end you will get a checklist you can use on real companies you come across.

Nothing you enter is saved or sent anywhere. Your answers stay on your device and disappear when you close this page.
